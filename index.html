Overview

Title: Rolling Railguns

Description: A rogue-like side scrolling shooter developed by Infrared Studios. The game was made in the Kaboom Javascript Engine/Library and published using Electron. The monsters have taken over the world of ROLLING RAILS and you have to beat them on each planet. You use many vehicles including: cars, bikes, planes and so much more.

Story and Design

Main Character: Playable Race Car driver who wields weapons on his vehicles. He is 5”8’, has brown hair and eyes, wears a racing helmet, racing suit, and has the ability to switch between vehicles in his garage.

Store Owner: The NPC to go to when the user needs vehicles or ammo.

John: The user's sidekick. He helps the user in all their journeys around the universe. He will help in times of need, like when the user has low HP. (John is only available in EASY MODE).

Storyline: 

  • Tutorial: Main Character is at his house in his garage. He sits down and turns on the news to see that extraterrestrial creatures have successfully taken control of the whole universe. He decides to get in his car and to go buy a turret to put on the back of his car. The car is a McLaren F1 from 1990. When he makes it to the shop, the owner provides the turret for free with a free RPG. He quickly heads home to upgrade his car. Monsters were spotted in the sky minutes away from his home, so he decides to hop in his car and explore a bit until running into some, which he uses the RPG turret mounted on his car to shoot them down. Once completed he picks up some of the items / parts the monsters' flying vehicles had dropped to take them home and upgrade his car. He adds boosters to the back of the car to go faster. This will be the end of the tutorial. All parts of it SHOULD INCLUDE controls / help on how to do these things. Even dialog from the characters included will be added.

  • Stage One (EARTH): The main character drives out of his garage in Gurnee, Illinois and starts driving EAST towards Lake Michigan. When he makes it to the coast in Waukegan, Illinois, he hits a park called North Beach. In the sky over the water he sees a group of flying vehicles from the monsters. The buildings are all destroyed around them once they make it to Waukegan. The sky turns red and monsters start flying around. The RPG is fired with the (“e”) button and has a short range. The ground turns bloody and the world starts raining lava; the character has to avoid it. Once they beat 30-35 monsters, the game will do the first stage of the boss.

  • Stage Two (HIP 13044 b (Andromeda Galaxy)): The main character lands here (HIP 13044 b) in a hypersonic speed space ship that transports him and his car. The planet is in perfect condition although there are monsters flying about. The user must spend about 3 minutes of their  time finding the code to unlock the gate to get new upgrades and attachments for their vehicle. After getting the code they are directed to the door in a massive silo. The silo houses thousands of monsters that fly out the second the user unlocks it. The user is prompted with a power-up that increases damage and blast range for the RPG. After claiming that the game begins to send monsters the users way. They must destroy about 80 before the game lets them move on
